1. Description of Service

Fud AI is a free, open-source nutrition tracking application available on iOS and Android. It uses AI to estimate the nutritional content of food from photos, nutrition labels (iOS), text descriptions, voice input, and manual entry. It also includes smart serving units such as slices, pieces, cups, or ml when the AI can infer them, optional food log sorting from the Home screen, weight + optional body-fat % tracking with goal lines and history charts on the Progress tab, an AI Coach chat with on-demand tool calling for personalized weight and nutrition guidance (Coach can fetch any date range of your weight, body-fat, calorie, or food history when you ask), Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets (iOS) / Glance widgets (Android), theme color personalization with matching app / launcher icons, app-update checks from About, bidirectional sync with Apple Health (iOS) and Health Connect (Android), and localization in 15 languages auto-selected by your phone's system language. The app supports Bring Your Own Key mode for 13 AI providers and 6 speech-to-text options. It may also offer Fud AI Plus on iOS, a paid no-key mode that routes AI and remote speech requests through Fud AI's serverless Gemini proxy with daily usage limits.

2. AI-Powered Estimates

Nutritional information, weight forecasts, and Coach responses provided by Fud AI are generated by your chosen AI provider and are estimated, not exact. These estimates should be used as general guidance only. Fud AI is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for dietary or weight-related decisions. The daily calorie target, BMR, TDEE, and macro split shown in the app are computed from peer-reviewed equations (Mifflin-St Jeor or Katch-McArdle for BMR, FAO/WHO/UNU activity multipliers for TDEE, Hall 2011 energy-balance math for goal targets). Full citations and source links are available in-app under Settings → Goals & Nutrition → Calculation Methods. Predictive equations carry inherent error (typically ±10% for BMR) and should not replace personalized clinical assessment.

3. AI Coach Chat Disclaimer

The AI Coach tab is a conversational assistant that reads your profile, weight log, food log, current date/timezone, and relevant meal details to produce suggestions. Its replies are generated by the third-party AI provider you select and may be inaccurate, biased, or outdated. Coach is not a substitute for advice from a dietitian, doctor, or other qualified professional. Do not rely on Coach for decisions about medical conditions, eating disorders, pregnancy, or any other health matter that warrants professional guidance.

4. AI Access, API Keys, and Plus

  • In Bring Your Own Key mode, you are responsible for obtaining and managing your own API keys from your chosen AI and speech-to-text providers.
  • BYOK API keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us — in iOS Keychain on iOS, in EncryptedSharedPreferences using AES-256 (Android Keystore-backed) on Android.
  • Usage, rate limits, and billing for BYOK AI/STT requests are between you and the provider you chose.
  • In Fud AI Plus mode, eligible requests are routed through Fud AI's serverless proxy to Google Gemini and are subject to the daily request limit shown in the app. Plus is intended for normal personal use, not automation, resale, scraping, or bulk API access.
  • You can switch between Plus and BYOK in app settings. BYOK remains available even if you do not subscribe to Plus.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

  • Fud AI Plus is optional. The free BYOK mode remains available without a Fud AI Plus subscription.
  • Fud AI Plus subscriptions are auto-renewable subscriptions on iOS, purchased through Apple App Store in-app purchase. Available plans, prices, billing periods, taxes, and currency are shown by Apple before purchase.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled through your Apple ID subscription settings before renewal. You can manage or cancel from iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, or from the App Store subscription management page.
  • Fud AI uses RevenueCat to verify, restore, and synchronize subscription entitlement status in the app.
  • Refunds, billing disputes, and App Store payment issues are handled by Apple under Apple's App Store policies.

6. Third-Party Providers

When you use the app in BYOK mode with an AI or STT provider, your requests (including food photos, voice audio, speech-language hints, and Coach messages) are sent directly from your device to that provider. When you use Fud AI Plus, eligible requests are sent through Fud AI's serverless proxy to Google Gemini. Each provider has its own terms and privacy policy that govern how they handle that data. You are responsible for reviewing and accepting those providers' terms. We do not control, endorse, or make representations about third-party providers.

Auto-routed providers (OpenRouter openrouter/free): The new free-tier OpenRouter default model auto-selects the upstream provider that handles your request — currently rotating among free vision endpoints from Baidu, Nvidia, and Google, with the available pool changing over time as OpenRouter updates its free tier. By selecting openrouter/free you accept that the upstream provider chosen by OpenRouter at request time is outside our control and may change without notice. If you require a fixed upstream, pick a specific provider model (e.g. google/gemini-2.5-flash, openai/gpt-4o) instead of the auto-routed default.

8. Health Disclaimer

Fud AI is a nutrition tracking tool, not a medical application. The app does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Nutritional estimates, BMR/TDEE values, macro targets, and weight forecasts are approximations based on published formulas (Katch-McArdle, Mifflin-St Jeor) and energy-balance math (7700 kcal ≈ 1 kg); they may not be accurate for all individuals. Users with medical conditions, eating disorders, or specific dietary needs should consult qualified healthcare professionals.

9. Apple Health & Health Connect Integration

By enabling Apple Health (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) integration, you authorize the app to read and write health data as described in our Privacy Policy. Written data includes body measurements (weight, height, body fat percentage) and per-meal nutrition (12 types: calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, monounsaturated fat, polyunsaturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium). The app also reads weight + body-fat samples back into your profile + Progress chart, so external readings from any other Health-compatible app or smart scale you've connected (Apple Watch, Withings, Renpho, Samsung Health, Fitbit, etc.) flow in automatically. On iOS, on first enable a one-shot historical backfill imports your past weight + body-fat samples so the chart starts populated. On iOS, you can revoke access via Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Fud AI; on Android, via the Health Connect app. Note that Delete All Data in the app only wipes local storage — your Apple Health / Health Connect samples persist unless you remove them separately.

11. Availability

The app requires an internet connection for AI food analysis, Coach chat, remote speech-to-text, Plus subscription checks/restores, and app-update checks. Fully offline operation is possible only when you use Ollama (local LLM) and the on-device speech recognizer (Native iOS or Native Android). Offline functionality otherwise is limited to viewing previously logged data, running local BMR/TDEE math, manual entry, reading from existing Apple Health / Health Connect samples, and re-logging from Saved Meals. Some Android OEM skins (OriginOS, MIUI/HyperOS, One UI) aggressively kill background alarms; the app exposes a Battery Optimization deep-link in Settings to whitelist Fud AI so scheduled reminders fire reliably — without whitelisting, reminders may be silently delayed or dropped.
